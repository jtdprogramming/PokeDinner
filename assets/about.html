Are you a new Pokemon trainer or enthusiast? Ever wondered how to keep your Pokemon in peak physical condition? Now you can find the best meal ideas for your Pokemon with a single search!.

Concept
  • PokeDinner is designed to help pokemon owners determine a fitting meal for their Pokemon. Food results based on their weight and caloric needs.
  • Motivation: To have fun learning to incorporate new technologies in a creative fashion, with the content focused on a recognizable established source.
    • User story:
  • AS A pokemon trainer/owner, I want to make sure I am feeding my pokemon an appropriate meal based on their size and caloric needs so that my pokemon maintains a healthy diet and lifestyle.
